Helping Hands Assistance Programs

Our approach is geared towards the achieving Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by basically transforming lives. These programs and projects include:

Food Packages

Food is a basic necessity for survival and also the key to a healthy life. Without regular healthy meals children struggle to go to school, carers struggle to work and communities are left battling to survive.

Health Care

During the blessed month of Ramadan, SHHI strives to provide food to as many people as possible to ensure that all fasting individuals are being nourished. Our target is to provide 1 Million Meals with the generosity of our supporters every Ramadan.

Water Access
