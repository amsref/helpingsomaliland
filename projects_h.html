Health Care Program

Good health is about more than just medicine and treatment; it includes all areas of life – that’s why we train communities on diet and health practices, and provide school meals to prevent child malnutrition. Once people have become ill however, access to affordable healthcare is struggle for most people. Somaliland Helping Hands plan to aleviate this situation by building hospitals and clinics, supply existing centres with life-saving medicines and clinical equipment, and provides ambulances to reach the most urgent cases of injury and illness.
