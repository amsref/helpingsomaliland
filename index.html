Partnership

Our approach to partnership is unique. We cultivate innovative collaborations and strategic partnerships with foundations and corporations that share our vision of investing in Somaliland to transform the future of the children, families and communities we serve. In collaboration with our partners, we work to positively address the unique and complex challenges facing some of Somaliland’s most vulnerable communities. We work with our partners to co-design programs on the ground that achieve measurable outcomes, ensuring that progress is executed, measured and replicated with success for lasting impact.

Mission, Values and Vision

Our mission is to work at the roots of communities to create environments in which they can thrive. Working alongside Somaliland communities to co-create a new story for the country - a future that is shaped by their values, powered by their own resourcefulness and built on their capabilities.

Our Values are to do exactly what’s needed, where it’s needed. This often means breaking the mould and seeking community-driven solutions.

Our Vision is of a Somaliland that is not dependent on aid, but on the resourcefulness and capabilities of its people.

Humility

Collaboration

Interconnection
