Somaliland Helping Hands Initiative (SHHI) is committed to providing equal opportunities to the African communities and making them stronger, more resilient, and self-sufficient. Through their own capabilities, SHHI is better equipped to thrive and withstand the impact of disasters. By making this generous donation today, you will join us in our commitment to support thriving and vibrant African communities. As a supporter, you will receive regular updates on our progress on the ground and alerts about urgent needs and crises faced by the communities.

Fundraise

Fundraising really is all about having fun whilst also helping those less fortunate around the world. You may already have an idea and just need some resources from us, or perhaps you really want to do something worthwhile but donâ€™t know where to start. Well here we have lots of fun and creative ideas along with helpful information to get you well on your way.

Fundraising Ideas

  • Auction - use social media to sell unwanted items
  • Bake Sale - sell other homemade delights alongside your delicious baked goods like milkshakes and smoothies
  • Football match or Tournament - organise a charity football tournament and ask all the players to make a donation on the day too.
  • Sports Day - Get your local community involved. Hold a sports day with the classic activities like an egg & spoon race, three-legged race, sack race and lots more.
  • Walk/Cycle to Work - Instead of driving to work or using public transport, use this fundraising idea to get into shape and donate the money you will save from buying tickets and filling up on petrol.
